Assign priority levels to your Execution Flows to manage their importance or urgency effectively.

To Set Priority to your Execution Flows:

*   Navigate to the **AI Pilots** from the left bar.

![Screen Shot 2025-05-31 at 9.34.43 PM.png](./Set Priority to your Execution Flows – Salesmate_files/Screen Shot 2025-05-31 at 9.34.43 PM.png)

*   Head Over to Execution Flows section
*   Now, click on Manage Priority option
*   A pop will appear where you can simply drag and drop the flows from the list to reorder them based on the Priority.
*   Once done click on **Save** the changes.
