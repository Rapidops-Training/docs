Salesmate Meeting Scheduler gives you the flexibility to enable or disable a meeting in case it has to be stopped for future booking or reinstated from a brief halt.

Meeting Scheduler can be marked as Active or Inactive whenever required as per your availability.

To**Disable**/**Enable**a Meeting Scheduler, follow the below-mentioned steps:


*   Navigate to the**More**option from left sidebar menu

*   Click on**Meeting Scheduler**![](./Enable or Disable Meeting Scheduler – Salesmate_files/mceclip0.png)


*   Hover over the**Meeting Name**which you wish to Enable/ Diable

*   Click on the**Action**button.

*   Hit**Disable**, to mark the meeting inactive for future bookings OR hit**Enable**, to activate back the Meeting Scheduler for upcoming bookings.

![](./Enable or Disable Meeting Scheduler – Salesmate_files/mceclip0(1).png)


***Color**identifications to identify Meeting Scheduler**Status*****Disabled**: Grey
    ***Enabled**: Green
