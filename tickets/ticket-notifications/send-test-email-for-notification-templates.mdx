---
title: "Send Test Email for Notification Templates"
---

Once the Ticket Notification Templates are customized you can send the Test email to see how the email will appear and what information it will include.

<Note>
  **Note:** Please note that this feature is only available on Paid plans
</Note>

To Send the Test Email for Ticket Notification Templates,

- Navigate to the **Profile Icon** on the top right corner
- Click on the **Set Up**
- Head over to the **Modules** category
- Click on the **Ticket** module

![Screenshot 2025-11-11 at 1.14.32 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-11at1.14.32PM.png)

- Head to the **Notifications** section, You will the List of the **Notification Templates**
- Hover on the preferred **Notification Template** and select the **Send Test Email** option under **Actions button.**

![Screenshot 2025-11-11 at 2.16.34 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-11at2.16.34PM.png)

- A pop will appear to enter the **Recipient** and the **Ticket** \* Once the information is added, click on **Send Test**

![Send Test Email Popup](images/003-send-test-email-popup-32005651889561.png)
