---
title: "How to Customize Ticket Notification Templates"
---

Skara provides users with the flexibility to personalize their Ticket Notification Templates according to their specific needs.

To Customize the Ticket Notification Templates,

- Navigate to the **Profile Icon** on the top right corner
- Click on the **Set Up**
- Head over to the **Modules** category
- Click on the **Ticket** module

![Screenshot 2025-11-11 at 1.14.32 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-11at1.14.32PM.png)

- Head to the **Notifications** section.
- You will see the List of the **_Notification Templates_**

![Screenshot 2025-11-11 at 1.16.39 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-11at1.16.39PM.png)

- Select the preferred Template 
  - A pop-up will appear, which allows you to customize the following: 
    - **Subject** 
    - **Body** (Make the required changes)
- Once done, you can either **Save & Preview** the Template to see how it will appear or just click on **Save** to save the changes

![Screenshot 2025-11-11 at 1.16.59 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-11at1.16.59PM.png)
