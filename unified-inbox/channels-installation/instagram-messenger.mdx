## Overview

**Instagram** is more than a Social Network. It is one of the biggest and most popular Sales channels these days. Salesmate provides a platform to integrate **Instagram** establishing a two-way channel of communication with your customers.

## Prerequisites

Before setting up Instagram Messenger integration, ensure you have:

- **Instagram Business Account** with admin access
- **Facebook Business Page** connected to your Instagram account
- **Active Unified Inbox subscription** in Salesmate
- **Admin permissions** on both Instagram and connected Facebook page

<Note>
  Instagram messaging integration requires a Facebook Business Page connection
  as Instagram uses Facebook's messaging infrastructure for business accounts.
</Note>

## Installation Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install Instagram Channel">
    1. Navigate to **Conversation Inbox** from the left sidebar
    2. Click on **Inbox Settings** at the bottom left
    3. Select **Channels** from the settings menu
    4. Click **Add New Channel**

    <img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/27524948637721" alt="Add new channel interface" />

  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Instagram Messenger">
    1. From the available channels list, select **Instagram Messenger**
    2. Click **Install** to begin the setup process
    3. You'll be redirected to Facebook's authorization page

    <img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/26118163075097" alt="Instagram Messenger channel selection" />
    <img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/26118432657817" alt="Instagram install popup" />

  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect to Instagram">
    1. Click on the **Connect to Instagram** option
    2. You will be redirected to the Facebook login page
    3. Enter your **Username and Password**

    <img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/26120051015193" alt="Facebook login page" />

    4. The system will fetch permissions for all the pages connected with the signed Facebook account

     <img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/27524935404953" alt="Select Instagram accounts" />
    5. Then select the Instagram accounts that you would like Salesmate to access

    <img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/27524935404953" alt="Select Instagram accounts" />

  </Step>

  <Step title="Business Access Selection">
    1. You will be redirected to the screen to select the Businesses that you would like Salesmate to Access
    2. You can select one of the following options:
        - **Opt in to all current and future businesses** - Select this option if you wish to connect the current and future Businesses
        - **Opt in to current businesses only** - Select this option if you wish to connect only the current Businesses

    <img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/27524948667289" alt="Business access selection" />

    3. Allow Salesmate the permission to access and hit on **Save**
    <img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/27524935420313" alt="Connection successful" />
    4. Once the information is saved, your account will then be connected successfully

    <img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/27524935428889" alt="Connection successful" />

  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Channel">
    1. Once the account is connected, you will be redirected to the **Configure** page
    2. Enable the Accounts you wish to configure
    3. Then hit on **Next**

    <img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/26124301329689" alt="Configure Instagram accounts" />

  </Step>

  <Step title="Channel Access">
    1. Once you configure the Messenger, you will be redirected to the Channel Access screen
    2. Here, you can grant access to:
        - **Invite Everyone to Chat:** This will allow everyone with a chat license to respond to the channel's conversations

    <img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/26124262824985" alt="Invite everyone to chat" />

        - **Invite selected Users:** This will allow only the selected Users with a chat license to respond to the channel's conversations

    <img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/26124301336345" alt="Invite selected users" />

    3. Once done click on **Finish**

    <img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/26124262836121" alt="Finish setup" />

  </Step>
</Steps>

### **Instagram-Specific Features**

- **Story Replies**: Handle replies to your Instagram Stories
- **Post Comments**: Manage comments on your Instagram posts (if enabled)
- **Rich Media Support**: Send images, videos, and carousel messages
- **Quick Replies**: Set up quick reply buttons for common customer actions
- **Story Mentions**: Get notified when your business is mentioned in stories

- **View Messages**: Access to read Instagram Direct Messages
- **Respond to Messages**: Permission to reply to customer messages

- **Email Notifications**: Receive email alerts for unresponded messages
- **Mobile Notifications**: Push notifications on mobile devices

## How to Uninstall Instagram Channel

<Steps>
  <Step title="Uninstall Instagram Channel">
    1. Navigate to the **Conversations** **Icon** from the left menu bar
    2. Select **Chats**
    3. Click **Chat Settings** from the bottom left corner
    4. Head to the **Channels** Option
    5. Here select **Instagram** and click on the three dots available at the corner right

    <img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/26124786291097" alt="Instagram uninstall options" />

    6. Select **Uninstall**
    7. **Confirm** removing the Channel and click on **Uninstall**

    <img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/26124786304409" alt="Confirm Instagram uninstall" />

  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  **Note**: Please note that once you uninstall the Instagram Channel, all the
  associated conversations will get deleted. This is an irreversible process.
</Note>

## Troubleshooting

### **Common Issues**

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Messages not syncing">
    **Possible solutions:**
    - Verify Instagram business account is properly connected to Facebook page
    - Check permissions for both Instagram and Facebook accounts
    - Refresh the connection by disconnecting and reconnecting
    - Ensure messaging is enabled on Instagram business profile
  </Accordion>

{" "}

<Accordion title="Unable to send messages">
  **Check these items:** - Verify team member has proper permissions - Ensure
  Instagram messaging is enabled for your business account - Check if the
  customer has restricted messages from your account - Verify the Facebook page
  connection is active
</Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Missing conversation history">
    **Troubleshooting steps:**
    - Check the date range in conversation filters
    - Verify the conversation wasn't archived or deleted
    - Ensure proper permissions to view conversation history
    - Check if messages were sent before the integration was set up
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
