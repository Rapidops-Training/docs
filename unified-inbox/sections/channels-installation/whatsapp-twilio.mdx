## Overview

Twilio's integration with WhatsApp enables businesses to leverage the popular messaging platform for communication. Salesmate now provides a platform to connect WhatsApp via Twilio for chat communication.

<Note>
**Note:**

- You must be registered to the **Twilio Paid Account** with **WhatsApp Senders** to Use WhatsApp Via Twilio. [Click here](https://www.twilio.com/docs/whatsapp/self-sign-up) to learn more about **Twilio WhatsApp Self-Sign-Up**.
- This integration is a part of the Salesmate Chat which requires the [Chat License](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/360059697531-Salesmate-Chats-Licenses-and-Cost)

</Note>

## Prerequisites

Before setting up WhatsApp by Twilio integration, ensure you have:

- **Twilio Paid Account** with WhatsApp Senders access
- **WhatsApp Business Account** approved by Twilio
- **Active Chat License** in Salesmate
- **Twilio Account SID and Auth Token**
- **WhatsApp number approved** by Twilio

<Warning>
  You must be registered to the **Twilio Paid Account** with **WhatsApp
  Senders** to use WhatsApp via Twilio. This integration is part of Salesmate
  Chat which requires the [Chat
  License](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/360059697531-Salesmate-Chats-Licenses-and-Cost).
</Warning>

## Installation Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Salesmate Channel Settings">
    1. Navigate to the **Conversations Icon** from the left menu bar
    2. Select **Chats**
    3. Click **Chat Settings** from the bottom left corner
    <img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/30702910005017" alt="Salesmate chat settings navigation" />
    4. Head to the **Channels** Option
    5. Click on the "**Add New Channel**"
    <img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/25832764376089" alt="Add New Channel" />

  </Step>

  <Step title="Select WhatsApp by Twilio">
    1. A popup will open up with available channel options
    2. Click the **Install** button for **WhatsApp by Twilio**

    <img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/25796446573465" alt="WhatsApp Twilio channel selection" />

  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect Your Twilio Account">
    1. Click on **Connect**
    <img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/25796731841561" alt="Twilio connection configuration" />
    2. Provide the following information to allow Salesmate to connect with Twilio:
       - **Account SID**: Account SID details from your Twilio Account
       - **Auth Token**: Auth Token from your Twilio Account
       - **WhatsApp Number**: A number approved by Twilio
    3. Once done, click on **Next**

    <img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/25792039744537" alt="Twilio connection configuration" />

  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Webhook Settings">
    1. You will be directed to the **Configure** screen
    2. Copy the Webhook URL provided
    3. Paste it to the appropriate Twilio fields
    4. Click on **Next**

    <img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/30705039723929" alt="Webhook configuration" />

  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Channel Access">
    1. You will be redirected to the **Channel Access** screen
    2. Choose one of the following options:
       - **Invite Everyone to Chat**: Allows everyone with a chat license to respond to conversations
      <img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/25793237589529" alt="Invite Everyone to Chat" />

       - **Invite Selected Users**: Allows only selected users with a chat license to respond
      <img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/25793253027609" alt="Invite Selected Users" />
    3. Configure your preferred access settings
    4. Click on **Finish** to complete the setup

    <img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/25793237600921" alt="Channel access configuration" />

  </Step>
</Steps>

## Channel Management

### **Access Control Options**

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Invite Everyone to Chat">
    This option allows everyone with a chat license to respond to the channel's conversations. Ideal for teams where multiple members need access to WhatsApp communications.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Invite Selected Users">
    This option allows only selected users with a chat license to respond to the channel's conversations. Perfect for maintaining controlled access to customer communications.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Uninstalling WhatsApp by Twilio

If you need to remove the WhatsApp by Twilio integration:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Channel Settings">
    1. Navigate to the **Conversations Icon** from the left menu bar
    2. Select **Chats**
    3. Click **Chat Settings** from the bottom left corner
    4. Head to the **Channels** Option
  </Step>

  <Step title="Remove the Channel">
    1. Select **WhatsApp by Twilio** from your channels list
    2. Click on the three dots available at the corner right
    3. Select **Uninstall**
    4. **Confirm** removing the Channel and click on **Uninstall**

    <img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/25874195931417" alt="Uninstall WhatsApp Twilio channel" />

  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Uninstalling the channel will permanently remove all WhatsApp integration
  settings. Make sure to backup any important configuration details before
  proceeding.
</Warning>
