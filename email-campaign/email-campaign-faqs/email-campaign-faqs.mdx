####**What happens if the List used as the recipient in the Campaign is deleted by the user?\*** If the list used in the Campaign is deleted by the User the campaign doesn’t execute and gets marked as failed. Once you open the campaign in edit mode, it shows you an error near the recipient section.

####**What happens if the\*\***View used as the recipient in the Campaign is deleted by the user?\*\*\* If the View used in the Campaign is deleted by the User the campaign doesn’t execute and gets marked as failed.
