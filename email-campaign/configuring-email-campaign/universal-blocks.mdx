Universal Blocks are reusable, brand-approved content snippets, such as headers, footers, legal disclaimers, or promotional banners, stored centrally for quick access and consistency across your email campaigns. It helps maintain brand consistency, reduce redundant design effort, and enable global content updates across templates.**Key Capabilities:***   Reusable in Campaign templates and Journey emails

*   Single-source editing that auto-updates across all instances

*   Customizable with variables and repeatable sections

*   Safe to update, with visibility into usage

How to create Universal Blocks


*   Navigate to your**Outreach**icon from the Left side bar

*   Click on**Template Library**![Screenshot 2025-08-03 at 12.59.39 PM.png](./Universal Blocks – Salesmate_files/Screenshot 2025-08-03 at 12.59.39 PM.png)


*   Now, head over to the**Universal Blocks**tab

*   Click on**New**to create a block

![Screenshot 2025-08-03 at 1.01.45 PM.png](./Universal Blocks – Salesmate_files/Screenshot 2025-08-03 at 1.01.45 PM.png)


*   Now, click on**Blank Block**to start creating your Universal Block

![Screenshot 2025-08-03 at 1.04.17 PM.png](./Universal Blocks – Salesmate_files/Screenshot 2025-08-03 at 1.04.17 PM.png)
