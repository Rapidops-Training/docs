###  How to Edit a Snippet

<Note>

**Note:** A user with edit snippet rights can perform this operation.

</Note>

To edit a Snippet,

- **Navigate to the** Profile Icon on the top right corner.

- **Click on the** Set Up.

- Head over to the **Library** category.

- **Click on the** Snippets option.

![Screen Shot 2024-12-02 at 12.29.05 PM.png](./images/Screen-Shot-2024-12-02-at-12.29.05-PM.png)

- **Hover on the Name of the Snippet you want to edit and click on** Actions .

- **Click on the** Edit option.

![Screen Shot 2024-12-02 at 1.24.12 PM.png](./images/Screen-Shot-2024-12-02-at-1.24.12-PM.png)

- **Make the changes you require and then hit the** Save Button.

![Edit html snippet.png](./images/blobid5.png)

###  How to Delete a Snippet

<Note>

**Note:** A user with delete snippet rights can perform this operation.

</Note>

To delete a Snippet,

- **Navigate to the** Profile Icon on the top right corner.

- **Click on the** Set Up.

- Head over to the **Library** category.

- **Click on the** Snippets option.

- **Hover on the Name of the Snippet you want to edit and click on** Actions .

- **Click on the** Delete option.

![Screen Shot 2024-12-02 at 2.22.52 PM.png](./images/Screen-Shot-2024-12-02-at-2.22.52-PM.png)

- Confirm your action to delete the Snippet.

![Screen Shot 2024-12-02 at 2.44.28 PM.png](./images/Screen-Shot-2024-12-02-at-2.44.28-PM.png)

<Note>

**Note:** Delete operations are irreversible; you can't recover your snippet once done.

</Note>
