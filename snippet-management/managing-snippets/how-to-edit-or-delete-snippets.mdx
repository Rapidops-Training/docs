You can easily edit or delete snippets in Salesmate CRM to keep your content accurate and relevant. In this article, we’ll show you how easy it is to edit or delete snippets so you can keep them updated and relevant to your needs.

- [How to Edit a Snippet](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/40451899848345-How-to-Edit-or-Delete-Snippets#h_01JDQJ2K8DCKT64AYVZW2BRD7Q)

- [How to Delete a Snippet](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/40451899848345-How-to-Edit-or-Delete-Snippets#01JDQK63SFZMTJC7NQZHQZWKZD)

### How to Edit a Snippet

<Note>
  **Note:** A user with edit snippet rights can perform this operation.
</Note>

To edit a Snippet,

- Navigate to the**Profile Icon**on the top right corner.

- Click on the**Set Up.\*** Head over to the**Library**category.

- Click on the**Snippets**option.

![Screen Shot 2024-12-02 at 12.29.05 PM.png](./How to Edit or Delete Snippets – Salesmate_files/Screen Shot 2024-12-02 at 12.29.05 PM.png)

- Hover on the Name of the Snippet you want to edit and click on**Actions**.

- Click on the**Edit**option.

![Screen Shot 2024-12-02 at 1.24.12 PM.png](./How to Edit or Delete Snippets – Salesmate_files/Screen Shot 2024-12-02 at 1.24.12 PM.png)

- Make the changes you require and then hit the**Save**Button.

![Edit html snippet.png](./How to Edit or Delete Snippets – Salesmate_files/blobid5.png)

### How to Delete a Snippet

<Note>
  **Note:**A user with delete snippet rights can perform this operation.
</Note>

To delete a Snippet,

- Navigate to the**Profile Icon**on the top right corner.

- Click on the**Set Up.\*** Head over to the**Library**category.

- Click on the**Snippets**option.

- Hover on the Name of the Snippet you want to edit and click on**Actions**.

- Click on the**Delete**option.

![Screen Shot 2024-12-02 at 2.22.52 PM.png](./How to Edit or Delete Snippets – Salesmate_files/Screen Shot 2024-12-02 at 2.22.52 PM.png)

- Confirm your action to delete the Snippet.

![Screen Shot 2024-12-02 at 2.44.28 PM.png](./How to Edit or Delete Snippets – Salesmate_files/Screen Shot 2024-12-02 at 2.44.28 PM.png)

<Note>
  **Note:**Delete operations are irreversible; you can't recover your snippet
  once done.
</Note>
