</Note>

Now add Salesmate Chat to your mobile apps directly and provide your customers an in-app chat experience.

- To install Chats to your Website, click here.
- To install chats to your iOS app, [click here](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/7972525508633).

Follow the below steps to navigate to the Chat installation page:

- Navigate to **Conversations Icon** on the left menu bar
- Click on **Chats**
- Head to **Inbox** **Settings** on bottom left.

![](./Installing Chats on your Android App – Salesmate_files/mceclip1.png)

- Here, click on **Channel** and then locate the **Salesmate Chat** channel. From the **Actions** menu, select **Edit**.

![](./Installing Chats on your Android App – Salesmate_files/mceclip2.png)

- Here, click on Installation and you will get 3 tabs Web, iOS, and Android, select the **Android** tab.
- Installation of Chat on your Android app helps sync live user data from your Android app to Salesmate, and lets you send in-app messages.

![](./Installing Chats on your Android App – Salesmate_files/mceclip0.png)

### Installing:

The android installation screen has two sections

- [**API Key**](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/7972447855001-Installing-Chats-on-your-Android-App#h_01G6QPRXD1AXSSPMJ1ANPDNJQ7)
- [**Push Notifications**](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/7972447855001-Installing-Chats-on-your-Android-App#h_01G6SNM99BDKBFHXCR4KY67MNA)

#### **API Key:**

You can use the below keys to add the Salesmate Chats to your app.

- **API Key:** A unique key for your Salesmate account.
- **Workspace ID:** A unique key for your Salesmate Chat workspace
- **Tenant ID:** This is your Salesmate domain link.

![](./Installing Chats on your Android App – Salesmate_files/mceclip1(1).png)

- The code is available in two languages
  - **Java**
  - **Kotlin**

![](./Installing Chats on your Android App – Salesmate_files/mceclip2(1).png)

#### **Enable Push Notifications:**

- To send push notifications through Messenger to your Android device, you need to configure the below settings.
- The push notifications are sent for newly received messages on Salesmate Messenger when visitor/contact leaves the app.

Here, you need to add the below details to the Salesmate Chat settings

- **Server API Key:** Add your server API key for Firebase Cloud Messaging
- **Firebase service account file (Only JSON file is supported)**

Once, you add the above information, the **Save** button would be enabled.
