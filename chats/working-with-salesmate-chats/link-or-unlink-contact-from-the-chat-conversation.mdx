With the Chat feature, you can now automatically associate a contact whenever a visitor initiates a chat. Similarly, you can also disassociate a contact from a conversation when necessary

**Topics Covered:**

*   [How to Link a Contact](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/29021410794009-Link-or-Unlink-Contact-from-the-Chat-Conversation#h_01HQ5AWTZPZG15DQ8X98CZ9B31)
*   [How to Unlink a Contact](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/29021410794009-Link-or-Unlink-Contact-from-the-Chat-Conversation#h_01HQ5BMWX4XAFF6MG0V4F9WWYA)

### How to Link a Contact

*   Navigate to the **Conversation** Icon on the left menu bar
*   Click on the **Chats** option 

<img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/50754448470041" alt="Salesmate chat navigation" />
*   Select the Chat that you wish to link the Contact for and click on Link Contact
*   You can either select a Contact to Link with the Conversation or simply create a New Contact

<img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/50754452857241" alt="Link contact from chat" />
### How to Unlink a Contact

*   Navigate to the **Conversation** Icon on the left menu bar
*   Click on the **Chats** option 

<img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/50754448470041" alt="Salesmate chat navigation" />
*   Select the **Chat** that you wish to **unlink the Contact**
*   Go to the **Contacts Widget** on the right and click on the **Actions** button
*   Select **Unlink Contact** from the dropdown

<img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/50754452857625" alt="Unlink contact from chat" />
*   Confirm the Action to continue to disassociate the Contact from the conversation

<img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/50754448472857" alt="Unlink contact confirmation" />
