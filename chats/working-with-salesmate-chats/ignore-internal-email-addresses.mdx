You can now add the email addresses to the ignore list that you want to ignore if included in **To** or **CC** recipients, as sometimes it may happen that your customers include one of your teammate’s email or companies email in **To** or **CC** which will be added as a participant in the conversation on inbox, but Now you can avoid that from happening.

### **How to ignore the company's internal email addresses?**

Just follow the below steps to add an email address to the ignore list:

*   Navigate to **Conversations Icon** on the left menu bar
*   Click on **Chats**
*   Head to **Chat Settings** on bottom left**.**

<img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/31719832783257" alt="Chat settings" />
*   Click on the **Email Forwarding** option in the left sidebar,
*   Switch to the tab **Ignore Internal Email Addresses**
*   Enter the email address in the given field that you want to avoid getting added to a conversation, if included in **To** or **CC**
*   Click on the **Add** button to add the email to the list.

<img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/31719900896921" alt="Ignore internal email addresses" />

Where,
*   You can also add the whole group of the company's emails by using ‘\*’ for example, \*@[your-domain.com](http://your-domain.com/)
*   You can also remove an email from the list by clicking on the Delete link button displayed in the table, and then confirm the action.

<img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/31719900910617" alt="Ignore internal email addresses list" />
*   Any emails added or removed will take effect immediately after the action is completed.

For more information on [Email Forwarding visit this page](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/4402552756365).
