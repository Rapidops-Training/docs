<Info>
Monthly cost: **`$15 / Month / User`**  
Yearly cost: **`$144 / Year / User`**
</Info>

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need to purchase a license for each user inside my Salesmate account?

You don't require to purchase a Salesmate Chats license for each user. You can only purchase the license for the users who need to converse with your customers via chat. 

[Learn more](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/360058877991) about how to assign or remove chat licenses.

### One of the employees left who was using a Chats license. Can I re-assign it to someone else?

Yes, you can always remove or assign licenses between users.
