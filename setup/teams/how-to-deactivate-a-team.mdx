<Note>**Note**: Only users with an "Admin" role can perform this action.

</Note>


### To Deactivate a Team :


*   Navigate to the**Profile icon**on the top right corner

*   Click on**Setup***   Head over to**Users and Security***   Click on**Teams**![](./How to Deactivate a Team_ – Salesmate_files/mceclip0.png)


*   You will find a list of already created teams(if created in prior).

*   Click on**Deactivate**to deactivate a team.

![](./How to Deactivate a Team_ – Salesmate_files/mceclip0(1).png)


*   Confirm**Yes**to**"Deactivate".**![](./How to Deactivate a Team_ – Salesmate_files/mceclip1.png)


*   Once deactivated the team will appear in the list of**"Inactive"**teams.

![](./How to Deactivate a Team_ – Salesmate_files/mceclip2.png)

<Note>

Please Note :


*   Deactivated teams can be "reactivated" again if required.

*   They will not appear for Team mentions.

</Note>
