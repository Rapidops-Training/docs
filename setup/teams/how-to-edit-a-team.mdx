### To Edit a Team :


*   Navigate to the**Profile icon**on the top right corner

*   Click on**Setup***   Head over to**Users and Security***   Click on**Teams**![](./How to Edit a Team_ – Salesmate_files/mceclip0.png)


*   You will find a list of already-created teams

*   Click on**Edit**to change/update the field values.

![](./How to Edit a Team_ – Salesmate_files/mceclip2.png)


*   You can update the fields as shown below.

*   Click on**Update**to save the changes.


![](./How to Edit a Team_ – Salesmate_files/mceclip3.png)
