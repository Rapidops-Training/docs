You can easily delete a Validation Rule to keep your system organized and relevant to your needs. In this article, we’ll show you how simple it is to delete a Validation Rule when it's no longer required.

You can refer to the article on creating a validation rule [here](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/42923331176601).


### How to Delete a Validation Rule


*   Navigate to the**Profile Icon**on the top right corner.

*   Click on**Set Up**.

*   Go to the**Modules**section.

*   Select the module where you want to delete a validation rule.

*   Click on the**Validation Rules**tab.

![Screen Shot 2025-02-18 at 11.27.52 AM.png](./How to Delete Validation Rule – Salesmate_files/Screen Shot 2025-02-18 at 11.27.52 AM.png)


*   Hover over the validation rule you want to delete and click on**Actions**.

*   Click on the**Delete**option.

![Screen Shot 2025-02-18 at 11.35.53 AM.png](./How to Delete Validation Rule – Salesmate_files/Screen Shot 2025-02-18 at 11.35.53 AM.png)


*   A confirmation message will appear:

    _“You are about to delete the {fieldName} validation rule. This action is irreversible. Do you want to continue?”_

    Click**Yes**to confirm.


![Screen Shot 2025-02-18 at 11.39.58 AM.png](./How to Delete Validation Rule – Salesmate_files/Screen Shot 2025-02-18 at 11.39.58 AM.png)


*   Once deleted, you will see a "_{fieldName}_ Validation rule successfully deleted" message.


![Screen Shot 2025-02-18 at 11.42.19 AM.png](./How to Delete Validation Rule – Salesmate_files/Screen Shot 2025-02-18 at 11.42.19 AM.png)

<Note>
