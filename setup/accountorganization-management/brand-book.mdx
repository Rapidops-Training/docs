The**Brand Boo**k feature will allow you to configure your organization's**colors, logos, and template styles.**With the existing option, we allow adding**Logos**to the brand book.Upload multiple logos to use across Salesmate modules, like**Meeting Scheduler, Chat, and Chat Journeys.**Currently, with the Brand Book option you can add the logos to the [Meeting Scheduler Custom Greetings](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/4674492918297) option.**Note:**This action can be performed by the user who has admin rights.Topics Covered in this Article:

[**Add a Logo**](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/14873359995929-Brand-Book#h_01GQM9SYBNZG4VEVSFW3DCB5VG)[**Update a Logo**](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/14873359995929-Brand-Book#h_01GQM9T6NFCD40GHD3T7FJWWN7)[**Delete a Logo**](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/14873359995929-Brand-Book#h_01GQM9TDCWJDMN3PHDA6WBZ7PJ)

### Add Logo

To add a Logo to the Brand Book, please follow the below steps:

Navigate to the**Profile icon**on the top right cornerClick on the**Setup**Head over to the**Organization**Click on the**Brand Book**

![mceclip0.png](./images/mceclip0.png)

Now, click on the**"+ Add Logo"**button to add the logo.

![mceclip1.png](./images/mceclip1.png)

This will open the below screen and will ask for the below information.**Logo Name:**Give a name to your logo, this name will be used to select the logo in Salesmate modules.**Logo Alt Text:**Give alternative text which should be displayed, in case the image doesnâ€™t appear.**Logo Redirection URL:**Embed URL to your logo that redirects the users to a particular webpage.**Upload Logo:**Here upload the image from your system.

![mceclip2.png](./images/mceclip2.png)

**Note:**Maximum size allowed 5MBSupported Image formats: .JPG, .JPEG & .PNG

### Update a Logo

To update a Logo to the Brand Book, please follow the below steps:

Navigate to the**Profile icon**on the top right cornerClick on the**Setup**Head over to the**Organization**Click on the**Brand Book**

![mceclip0.png](./images/mceclip0.png)

Now open the Logo from the list that you wish to update.Here, you can now update the**Logo name, Logo Alt text, and Redirection URL**. To change the logo image click on the**Change**button and upload the new image.Once done, hit on**Save**

![mceclip3.png](./images/mceclip3.png)

### Delete a Logo

To delete a Logo to the Brand Book, please follow the below steps:

Navigate to the**Profile icon**on the top right cornerClick on the**Setup**Head over to the**Organization**Click on the**Brand Book**

![mceclip0.png](./images/mceclip0.png)

Open the Logo from the list that you wish to Delete and click on the**Delete icon**Upon clicking on the**Delete icon**, it will show a confirmation popup whether you wish to delete the logo or not.

![mceclip4.png](./images/mceclip4.png)

Confirm the**Delete**

![mceclip5.png](./images/mceclip5.png)

**Note:**Deleting a logo will remove it permanently, and it will be removed from all places where it has been used.
