To add colors to the Tag,

Navigate to the **Profile Icon** on the top right cornerClick on **Set Up** Head over to the **Misc** categoryClick on Tag Management

![Screenshot 2023-10-05 at 2.54.21 PM.png](./images/Screenshot-2023-10-05-at-2.54.21-PM.png)

###  Creating a New Tag

Click on the Add Tag option

![Screenshot 2023-10-05 at 3.00.18 PM.png](./images/Screenshot-2023-10-05-at-3.00.18-PM.png)

Click on the **Square icon**

![mceclip0.png](./images/mceclip0-17.png)

Select the **Colour**

![mceclip1.png](./images/mceclip1-19.png)

Enter the **Tag Name** Click on **Save**

![Screenshot 2023-10-05 at 3.08.04 PM.png](./images/Screenshot-2023-10-05-at-3.08.04-PM.png)

###  Update the Existing Tags

Select the Tag you wish to **Update** Click on the **Edit** option

![Screenshot 2023-10-05 at 3.10.26 PM.png](./images/Screenshot-2023-10-05-at-3.10.26-PM.png)

Click on the **Square Icon** followed by selecting the ColorHit on the **Update** option

![mceclip2.png](./images/mceclip2-14.png)
