Tags are a versatile tool to keep your Data segregated, they help organize your Records by different segments and enable data-driven decision-making. Salesmate now allows you to customize the Tags with the color of your choice.

You can add colors while,


*   [**Creating a New Tag**](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/23694312559257#h_01HBZJJ4HSDX444R1FB9W95NMQ)

*   [**Update the Existing Tags**](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/23694312559257#h_01HBZK9W8YXJK8BT4FM8QTECF7)

To add colors to the Tag,


*   Navigate to the**Profile Icon**on the top right corner

*   Click on**Set Up***   Head over to the**Misc**category

*   Click on Tag Management

![Screenshot 2023-10-05 at 2.54.21 PM.png](./Tag Color Customization – Salesmate_files/Screenshot 2023-10-05 at 2.54.21 PM.png)


### Creating a New Tag


*   Click on the Add Tag option

![Screenshot 2023-10-05 at 3.00.18 PM.png](./Tag Color Customization – Salesmate_files/Screenshot 2023-10-05 at 3.00.18 PM.png)


*   Click on the**Square icon**![](./Tag Color Customization – Salesmate_files/mceclip0.png)


*   Select the**Colour**![](./Tag Color Customization – Salesmate_files/mceclip1.png)


*   Enter the**Tag Name***   Click on**Save**![Screenshot 2023-10-05 at 3.08.04 PM.png](./Tag Color Customization – Salesmate_files/Screenshot 2023-10-05 at 3.08.04 PM.png)


### Update the Existing Tags


*   Select the Tag you wish to**Update***   Click on the**Edit**option

![Screenshot 2023-10-05 at 3.10.26 PM.png](./Tag Color Customization – Salesmate_files/Screenshot 2023-10-05 at 3.10.26 PM.png)


*   Click on the**Square Icon**followed by selecting the Color

*   Hit on the**Update**option
