Once you create a Custom Field, you also get options to Edit, Delete, or move the Field to the Unused section

Topics Covered:


*   [How to Edit Custom Fields](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/360041829992#h_01HAVE65CNRJC8WB3TP69X6586)

*   [How to Delete Custom Fields](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/360041829992#h_01HQSQAV5SS2VMB0QFZCQ6THKZ)


### How to Edit Custom Fields

To Edit Custom Fields


*   Navigate to the**Profile Icon**on the top right corner

*   Click on the**Set Up***   Head Over to the**Modules**Category

*   Select the Module(**Contact, Company, Deal, Activities, Products, and Custom Modules)**![](./How do I Edit_ Delete Custom fields – Salesmate_files/mceclip1.png)


*   Click on**Customize the Create Module Form**under Layout

![](./How do I Edit_ Delete Custom fields – Salesmate_files/mceclip2.png)


*   Select the Field that you wish to Edit, then click on the three dots located at the corner

*   Click on the**Edit Field**option

![](./How do I Edit_ Delete Custom fields – Salesmate_files/mceclip0.png)

OR


*   Go to**All Detail Form Field**Section

*   Select the Field to**Edit**, then hover over the field you wish to modify and click on the**Actions**button.

![](./How do I Edit_ Delete Custom fields – Salesmate_files/mceclip1(1).png)


### How to Delete Custom Fields

Similarly to Delete the Fields, follow the above-mentioned steps and select the Delete option,


*   To Delete from**Custom Create Forms**, select the field you wish to Delete and click on the**Delete Field**option

![](./How do I Edit_ Delete Custom fields – Salesmate_files/mceclip0(1).png)


*   To Delete From**All Detail Form Fields**, hover over the field you wish to modify and click on the**Actions**button.

![](./How do I Edit_ Delete Custom fields – Salesmate_files/mceclip1(2).png)


*   A pop-up will appear with an option to [Move the Fields to Unused Section](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/29324541435289-How-to-Move-Custom-Fields-to-Unused-Section) or Delete the Field

*   Click on the**Delete Field**option

![](./How do I Edit_ Delete Custom fields – Salesmate_files/mceclip2(1).png)


*   Confirm the action to continue deleting the field

![](./How do I Edit_ Delete Custom fields – Salesmate_files/mceclip3.png)

<Note>
**Note:**Please note that the System Fields cannot be deleted
</Note>
