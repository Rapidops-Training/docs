A**Navigation Sidebar**is a user interface element that provides easy access to various features, modules, and sections of the CRM.

By default, the menu sidebar displays a set of options in a fixed order. However, you can customize the order of these options to better suit your Organization’s needs.

To Customize the Sidebar,


*   Navigate to the**Profile Icon**in the top right corner.

*   Click on**Set-Up***   Head over to the**Misc**category

*   Select the**Customize Sidebar**option

![Screenshot 2023-09-06 at 11.09.26 AM.png](./How to Customize the Sidebar – Salesmate_files/Screenshot 2023-09-06 at 11.09.26 AM.png)


*   Here you can change the order of the Module with simple drag and drop.

![Screenshot 2023-09-06 at 3.27.35 PM.png](./How to Customize the Sidebar – Salesmate_files/Screenshot 2023-09-06 at 3.27.35 PM.png)


*   The Option in a Sidebar with multiple options is called a**Group**.

![Screenshot 2023-09-06 at 3.32.18 PM.png](./How to Customize the Sidebar – Salesmate_files/Screenshot 2023-09-06 at 3.32.18 PM.png)


*   You can create a new group from the**Add Group**option mentioned in the top right.

![Screenshot 2023-09-06 at 3.34.29 PM.png](./How to Customize the Sidebar – Salesmate_files/Screenshot 2023-09-06 at 3.34.29 PM.png)


*   Enter the appropriate**Group Name**and select the**Group Icon**.

*   Click on**Save**.

![Screenshot 2023-09-06 at 4.35.11 PM.png](./How to Customize the Sidebar – Salesmate_files/Screenshot 2023-09-06 at 4.35.11 PM.png)


*   You can**Edit**the Group by clicking on the three dots given in the top right corner of any Group.

*   Select the**Edit**option

![Screenshot 2023-09-06 at 3.38.21 PM.png](./How to Customize the Sidebar – Salesmate_files/Screenshot 2023-09-06 at 3.38.21 PM.png)


*   You can**Edit**the**Group Name**and the**Group Icon**, followed by an**Update**.

![Screenshot 2023-09-06 at 4.48.29 PM.png](./How to Customize the Sidebar – Salesmate_files/Screenshot 2023-09-06 at 4.48.29 PM.png)


*   If you want to reset the order of the menu items to the system default, then click on**“Reset to default”.**![Screenshot 2023-09-06 at 4.54.53 PM.png](./How to Customize the Sidebar – Salesmate_files/Screenshot 2023-09-06 at 4.54.53 PM.png)

<Note>
**Note:**You cannot delete a group that contains any menu items inside it, the option to delete will be shown disabled.
</Note>
