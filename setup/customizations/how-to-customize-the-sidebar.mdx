---
title: "How to Customize the Sidebar"
---
By default, the menu sidebar displays a set of options in a fixed order. However, you can customize the order of these options to better suit your Organization’s needs.

<iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/pSkWbLWss3c?list=PLyYol_VsdQ5kq3RxvfA2NaXeI_5bAaF1x" title="YouTube video" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture; web-share" allowfullscreen/>

To Customize the Sidebar,

Navigate to the **Profile Icon** in the top right corner.Click on **Set-Up** Head over to the **Misc** categorySelect the **Customize Sidebar** option

![Screenshot 2023-09-06 at 11.09.26 AM.png](./images/Screenshot-2023-09-06-at-11.09.26-AM.png)

Here you can change the order of the Module with simple drag and drop.

![Screenshot 2023-09-06 at 3.27.35 PM.png](./images/Screenshot-2023-09-06-at-3.27.35-PM.png)

The Option in a Sidebar with multiple options is called a **Group**.

![Screenshot 2023-09-06 at 3.32.18 PM.png](./images/Screenshot-2023-09-06-at-3.32.18-PM.png)

You can create a new group from the **Add Group** option mentioned in the top right.

![Screenshot 2023-09-06 at 3.34.29 PM.png](./images/Screenshot-2023-09-06-at-3.34.29-PM.png)

Enter the appropriate **Group Name** and select the **Group Icon**. Click on **Save**.

![Screenshot 2023-09-06 at 4.35.11 PM.png](./images/Screenshot-2023-09-06-at-4.35.11-PM.png)

You can **Edit** the Group by clicking on the three dots given in the top right corner of any Group.Select the **Edit** option

![Screenshot 2023-09-06 at 3.38.21 PM.png](./images/Screenshot-2023-09-06-at-3.38.21-PM.png)

You can **Edit** the **Group Name** and the **Group Icon**, followed by an **Update**.

![Screenshot 2023-09-06 at 4.48.29 PM.png](./images/Screenshot-2023-09-06-at-4.48.29-PM.png)

If you want to reset the order of the menu items to the system default, then click on **“Reset to default”.**![Screenshot 2023-09-06 at 4.54.53 PM.png](./images/Screenshot-2023-09-06-at-4.54.53-PM.png)

- *Note:**You cannot delete a group that contains any menu items inside it, the option to delete will be shown disabled.
