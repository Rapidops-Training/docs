An email template is a pre-defined email layout, that may already include content like images or text. Rather than create a new email from scratch each time, you can use a template as a base. Templates are also handy for making sure email designs follow brand guidelines.

### How to Create an Email Template

To create email templates:


*   Navigate to the**More Icon**from the left Menu bar

*   Click on**Email Templates***   Head over to**New Template**to create a template.

![](./How to Create Email Templates – Salesmate_files/mceclip0.png)

Here add the following details to start creating a New Template,


***Template Name:**Add a name to the template for using it while you send the emails

***Folder:**Manage your email templates based on your requirement

***Subject:**Add the purpose of the email

***Body:**Add email content and use attributes to personalize the emails

![](./How to Create Email Templates – Salesmate_files/mceclip1.png)


### Text Editor Options

Use a different text editor option to customize the email content


***More Text:**With this option, you can customize the content by changing**Font Style, Font Size, Font Family, Text color, and Background color**. You can also**Underline the Content**,**Use Strikethrough**, and also**Clear Formatting**from here.

![](./How to Create Email Templates – Salesmate_files/mceclip2.png)


***More Paragraph:**With this option, you can align the content to the Left, Right, or Center. Furthermore, you can also use the Order or Unorder List for alignment.

![](./How to Create Email Templates – Salesmate_files/mceclip3.png)


***Insert Link:**This option allows you to add any links to your content.

![](./How to Create Email Templates – Salesmate_files/mceclip4.png)


***Insert Image:**This option allows you to add any image or gif to personalize your content by simply adding the links or using the Media Manager

![](./How to Create Email Templates – Salesmate_files/mceclip5.png)


***Insert table:**You can use this option to add content in table format.

![](./How to Create Email Templates – Salesmate_files/mceclip6.png)


***Meeting Scheduler:**With this option, you can add Meeting links to your content.

![](./How to Create Email Templates – Salesmate_files/mceclip11.png)


***Insert Attributes:**Insert attributes to personalize your content

![](./How to Create Email Templates – Salesmate_files/mceclip10.png)


***Code View:**View email in the HTML editor

![](./How to Create Email Templates – Salesmate_files/mceclip9.png)


*   Click on**Save**to save the template
