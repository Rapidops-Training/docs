title: "Inserting Numeric Operators in Email Template using liquid js"
---
You can insert numeric functions in the Email Template to **Add, Subtract, Divide,**and **multiply** Salesmate fields.

###  **Topics covered:**

- [**Plus**](#plus)

- [**Minus**](#minus)

- [**Times**](#times)

- [**Divided By**](#divided-by)

###  Plus

This function adds a number to the field mentioned by you.

- **Input:** 
```highlight
| The total sum is : &#123;&#123;80 | plus:10&#125;&#125; |
```

![mceclip1.png](./images/mceclip1-4.png)

- **Output:** 
```highlight
The total sum is : 90
```

![mceclip4.png](./images/mceclip4-2.png)
