When you are reaching out to the contacts, it is valuable to provide them with the most information about you, your company, and how to contact you for more information.

To handle this efficiently, Salesmate suggests creating a specific email signature for your email account, so that every customer receives the information necessary to help you close the sale.**Topics Covered:\*** [Adding your Signature](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/360002748811-How-do-I-set-up-my-email-signature-in-Salesmate#h_01HRXY3DDVMRFSKA2Q05GYP45S)

- [Adding Multiple Signatures](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/360002748811-How-do-I-set-up-my-email-signature-in-Salesmate#h_01J2XV3Y9W7CJRNEFKS52QWNPC)

### Adding your Signature

To add your email signature in Salesmate,

- Navigate to**the Profile icon**on the top right corner

- Click on**Go to my Account.\*** Under the**General Settings**towards the end of the page add your Email Signature

![](./How do I set up my email signature in Salesmate\_ – Salesmate_files/mceclip1.png)

### Adding Multiple Signatures

- Navigate to**the Profile icon**on the top right corner

- Click on**Go to my Account.\*** Head over to the**Email Settings\*** Click on the**Email Signature**![](./How do I set up my email signature in Salesmate\_ – Salesmate_files/mceclip0.png)

- Click on**\+ Add**option to add new signature

![](./How do I set up my email signature in Salesmate\_ – Salesmate_files/mceclip1(1).png)

- Enter the**Signature Name**and hit on**Save**![](./How do I set up my email signature in Salesmate\_ – Salesmate_files/mceclip2.png)

- Once done you can create a Signature of your choice and mark one as **Default**

![](./How do I set up my email signature in Salesmate\_ – Salesmate_files/mceclip3.png)

<Note>
**Note:**

- Select the position of the email signature - "**Place this signature before quoted text in replies**"
- HTML is supported by the email signature too. You can do so by adding the HTML source via **`</>`** button.
- The selected Default Signature would be used in Emails

</Note>
