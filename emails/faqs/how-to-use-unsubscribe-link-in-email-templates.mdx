You can add an**unsubscribe**link to your emails using templates that are sent from the Contact / Activity / Deal page or using compose email in Inbox or Gmail Chrome extensions and mobile applications.

This link allows recipients to opt out of receiving such emails from your account. Adding unsubscribe links to your email communications can help you move toward compliance with spam laws (e.g., CAN-SPAM, GDPR, CASL) and improve email deliverability by allowing unengaged recipients a way to opt-out.

You can Add the Unsubscribe links to the,

- [Email Templates](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/360037950511#h_01HS85FD9BCBGATQAMF4HVH58K)

- [Campaign Templates](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/360037950511#h_01GWKNWFYPVWSVDGKFX99KSJ1N)

### Email Templates

To Add the Unsubscribe links to the Email Templates,

- To create email templates,

- Navigate to the**More Icon**on the left menu bar.

- Click on**Email Templates**.

- You can choose to Add the Unsubscribe link to Templates already created or click on**New Template**to add it in a new template.

![](./How to use Unsubscribe link in Email Templates – Salesmate_files/mceclip0.png)

- To add the Unsubscribe link here, click on attributes and select the Unsubscribe option under Contacts.

![](./How to use Unsubscribe link in Email Templates – Salesmate_files/mceclip0(1).png)

- It will be inserted as a variable in the Email body.

![](./How to use Unsubscribe link in Email Templates – Salesmate_files/mceclip1.png)

- Copy the attribute, cut - `{{Contact.unsubscribe}}`, type in the**Unsubscribe,**and go to insert a link.

- Now paste the copied attribute to the URL and click on**Insert.**![](./How to use Unsubscribe link in Email Templates – Salesmate_files/mceclip2.png)

- Once you click on Insert**Unsubscribe**will now appear in your email body**.**![](./How to use Unsubscribe link in Email Templates – Salesmate_files/mceclip3.png)

### Campaign Templates

To Add the Unsubscribe links to the Campaign Templates,

- With this option, you can create a new template or can select the predefined templates.

![](./How to use Unsubscribe link in Email Templates – Salesmate_files/mceclip4.png)

- To add the Unsubscribe link here, drag and drop the Text block to the editor.

- You can add the Unsubscribe link by selecting the**Unsubscribe**option given in the toolbar.

![](./How to use Unsubscribe link in Email Templates – Salesmate_files/mceclip7.png)

- When the contact receives the email and hits the**Unsubscribe**link, upon being not interested in the offered service.

- The contact will be redirected to an unsubscribe page where he can select the reason for unsubscribing.

  - I no longer want to receive these emails
  - I never signed up for this mailing list
  - The emails are inappropriate
  - The emails are spam and should be reported
  - Other (fill in the reason below)

- The contact will receive a success message on selecting and submitting the option.

- Under Salesmate Two Fields will be updated:

**\*Email Opt-Out:**True

**_Email Opt-Out Reason_** The Email field will be updated with**red color**as identification that the contact has opted out from receiving future emails.
