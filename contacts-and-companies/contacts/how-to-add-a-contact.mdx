Contacts form an integral part of any Sales agent's day-to-day business interactions. They store various everlasting information related to the contact right from their mobile number, alternate phone number, email address, twitter account, facebook account, etc.

- Contact can be a member of a**Company.\*** Contacts can be your**Lead**, Enquiries which can be categorized under contacts.

- Contact can have multiple deals open in the system at a particular point in time.**Contacts can be added to the system by various methods:\*\*\***Contacts\*\*> New ( Contact Listing Page )

![Screen Shot 2024-02-29 at 5.12.44 PM.png](./How to add a Contact\_ – Salesmate_files/Screen Shot 2024-02-29 at 5.12.44 PM.png)

**\*Company**> Add Associated Contact ( Add a Company Popup )

![Screen Shot 2024-02-29 at 5.38.48 PM.png](./How to add a Contact\_ – Salesmate_files/Screen Shot 2024-02-29 at 5.38.48 PM.png)

**\*Activity**> Contact > Create New Contact ( Quick create a Contact )

![Screen Shot 2024-02-29 at 5.57.51 PM.png](./How to add a Contact\_ – Salesmate_files/Screen Shot 2024-02-29 at 5.57.51 PM.png)

**\*Deal**> Contact > Create New Contact ( Quick create a Contact )
