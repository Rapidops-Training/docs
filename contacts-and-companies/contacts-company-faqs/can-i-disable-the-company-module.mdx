title: "Can I disable the Company module?"
description: "Yes, Salesmate now allows you to disable the Company module."
---

Yes, Salesmate now allows you to disable the Company module.

To disable the Company module,


*   Navigate to the**Profile Icon**on top right corner

*   Go to**Set Up***   Head over to**Modules**category.

*   Select the**Company**Module
