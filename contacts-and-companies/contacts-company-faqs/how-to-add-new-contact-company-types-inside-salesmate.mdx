---
title: "How to Add New Contact/ Company Types inside Salesmate?"
---
A "Contact Type" is a classification or categorization that helps organize and manage different kinds of contacts within the database.

<Note>

- **Note:** You will require to manage field rights to perform this action.

</Note>

To add a New Contact Type:

- **Navigate to the** Profile Icon on the top right corner

- **Click on the** Set Up

- Head Over to the **Modules** Category

- **Select the** Module (Contact, Company)

![](./images/mceclip0.png)

- **Go to the** All Detail Form Fields section

- **Search for** Type field and hover over it, Action button will appear

- **Click on the** Edit Field option

![](./images/mceclip0.png)

- **A pop up will open to** Add New Value , simple add the new Type here and hit on

- **Save**.

![](./images/mceclip0.png)
