## Check Team Availability

Follow the steps below to view your team members' availability:

1. Click on the **More** option next to **Inbox Settings**.
2. Select **Team Availability**.

![Team Availability Navigation](/Screenshot2025-10-07at1.08.34AM.png)

3. Use the search bar to find specific users or apply filters based on their status.
