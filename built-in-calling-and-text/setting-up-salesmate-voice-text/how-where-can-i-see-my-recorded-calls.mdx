Once you have enabled call recording for all inbound and outbound calls for a number. The call recordings will be logged.You can either view all the call recordings on the [Dashboard](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/360004731292-How-Where-can-I-see-my-recorded-calls#h_01GD0BHADNS1AR20BX72TH2RK3) or an [Activity](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/360004731292-How-Where-can-I-see-my-recorded-calls#h_01GD0BGYB5GPSRND1SE3ERZZXA) for the contact.

### Dashboard

Navigate to**Dashboard.**Click on**Add widget**in the top right corner.

![mceclip0.png](./images/mceclip0.png)

Add the**Call Logs**Report.

![mceclip1.png](./images/mceclip1.png)

The Call Logs report can be filtered Sales rep-wise and for the duration you desire.

![mceclip1(1).png](./images/mceclip11.png)

### Activity

Navigate to the**contact**. Click on**Activities**on the contact timeline.Here you can view all the recordings associated with the contact.

![Screenshot 2025-06-23 at 11.13.24 AM.png](./images/Screenshot-2025-06-23-at-11.13.24-AM.png)
