Once you have enabled call recording for all inbound and outbound calls for a number. The call recordings will be logged.

You can either view all the call recordings on the [Dashboard](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/360004731292-How-Where-can-I-see-my-recorded-calls#h_01GD0BHADNS1AR20BX72TH2RK3) or an [Activity](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/360004731292-How-Where-can-I-see-my-recorded-calls#h_01GD0BGYB5GPSRND1SE3ERZZXA) for the contact.

### Dashboard

*   Navigate to **Dashboard.**
*   Click on **Add widget** in the top right corner. 

![](./How _ Where can I see my recorded calls_ – Salesmate_files/mceclip0.png)

*   Add the **Call Logs** Report.

![](./How _ Where can I see my recorded calls_ – Salesmate_files/mceclip1.png)

*   The Call Logs report can be filtered Sales rep-wise and for the duration you desire.

![](./How _ Where can I see my recorded calls_ – Salesmate_files/mceclip1(1).png)

### Activity

*   Navigate to the **contact**. 
*   Click on **Activities** on the contact timeline.
*   Here you can view all the recordings associated with the contact.
