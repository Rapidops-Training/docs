In Salesmate, to be able to stop calling out of the app using Salesmate Calling. You will need to deactivate the app. Here's how to do it : 

*   Navigate to the **Profile Icon** on the top right corner
*   Click on **Setup**
*   Head over to **Voice & Text** category
*   Click on **Voice Apps**

![](./How do you disable_ remove the calling app_ – Salesmate_files/mceclip1.png)

*   Click on **Remove.**

![Screenshot_2023-05-31_at_12.46.37_PM.png](./How do you disable_ remove the calling app_ – Salesmate_files/Screenshot 2023-05-31 at 12.46.37 PM.png)

*   You will be **prompted to Confirm** if you want to disable the Salesmate Voice & Text app.

![](./How do you disable_ remove the calling app_ – Salesmate_files/mceclip0.png)

<Note>
**Note:** Please note this action is irreversible. Contact Support to enable Calling in your account.
</Note>
