Customers might call you for a number of reasons. They might want a trial period extension, new features, or simply have their calls returned. To help you engage, prioritize, and manage calls better, Salesmate lets you take notes during the call and create activities soon after your call has ended.

*   [Logging an Outgoing Call](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/360004785111-How-do-you-create-an-activity-after-or-during-a-call#h_01GJ5H2CE2NS425QVAK42NJARH)

### Logging an Incoming Call

To log an incoming call after the call is completed, 

*   If the phone number of the contact is added in Salesmate then it will display the contact name when the incoming call comes.

![Screen_Shot_2023-04-10_at_3.25.51_PM.png](./How do you create an activity after or during a call_ – Salesmate_files/Screen Shot 2023-04-10 at 3.25.51 PM.png)

*   Select the **Outcome** of the Call from the Outcome list. 

![](./How do you create an activity after or during a call_ – Salesmate_files/mceclip0.png)

*   Click on **Log as a New Activity**. 
*   You can also select the option to Log the **Activity Manually**

![](./How do you create an activity after or during a call_ – Salesmate_files/mceclip1.png)

*   The Incoming calls are logged as **'Inbound call with {Contact Name}'.**

![mceclip4.png](./How do you create an activity after or during a call_ – Salesmate_files/mceclip4.png)

*   You can view the activity on the timeline of the contact. 

![mceclip5.png](./How do you create an activity after or during a call_ – Salesmate_files/mceclip5.png)

### Logging an Outgoing Call

You can log your call with relevant notes and Outcome once the call to your customer is completed, To log the Outgoing Call, 

*   Select the **Outcome** of the Call from the Outcome list. 

![](./How do you create an activity after or during a call_ – Salesmate_files/mceclip2.png)

*   Click on **Log as a New Activity**. 
*   You can also select the option to Log the **Activity Manually.** 

![](./How do you create an activity after or during a call_ – Salesmate_files/mceclip3.png)

*   The Outgoing calls are logged as **'Outbound call with {Contact Name}'.**

**![mceclip6.png](./How do you create an activity after or during a call_ – Salesmate_files/mceclip6.png)**

*   You can view the logged activity on the timeline of the contact. 

**![mceclip7.png](./How do you create an activity after or during a call_ – Salesmate_files/mceclip7.png)**

### Logging a Missed Call

**Timeline view :** 

  
![mceclip0__2_.png](./How do you create an activity after or during a call_ – Salesmate_files/mceclip0 (2).png)  
  

Also, while you are unavailable you will find an activity **'Missed Call - From {Contact Name}'** under your **All Open Activities.**

![mceclip3__3_.png](./How do you create an activity after or during a call_ – Salesmate_files/mceclip3 (3).png)

<Note>


**Note:** In case there is no name for the contact, the activity will be logged as 'Missed Call - From {Contact.Number}'


</Note>
