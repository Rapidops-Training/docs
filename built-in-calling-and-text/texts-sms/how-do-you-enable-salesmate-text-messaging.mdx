To be able to send text messages out of Salesmate, you will need to enable the Salesmate Voice & Text app.

<Note>


[**Click Here - Follow the Article linked to know more about the Calling Activation Process**](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/360004733011) 


</Note>

#### **Calling and Text Feature Activation Steps :**

*   Navigate to the **Profile Icon** on the top right corner
*   Click on **Setup**
*   Head over to **Voice & Text** category
*   Click on **Voice Apps**

![](./How do you enable Salesmate Text Messaging – Salesmate_files/mceclip0.png)

*   Click on **Install** (Salesmate Voice & Text)

![](./How do you enable Salesmate Text Messaging – Salesmate_files/mceclip1.png)

**Fill in the Request Form :** 

*   (Please provide all accurate details for quick activation of this service. Request with missing or invalid information is liable to rejection.)

![](./How do you enable Salesmate Text Messaging – Salesmate_files/mceclip2.png)

*   Once Activated, you need to Purchase a number that has the **Capability** to allow **Text Messaging**.

Refer to the capability column while purchasing the number to do so.

![](./How do you enable Salesmate Text Messaging – Salesmate_files/mceclip3.png)
