### **Conversational View** 

Communication isn’t a one-way street. To ensure you have effective two-way communication, Salesmate CRM has added the conversation view to its text messages. 

To view Conversation Screen, follow the below steps:

*   Navigate to **Left Menu** Bar and click the **Text** Icon 

You would find below options available under the View:

*   **All**
*   **Inbox**
*   **Scheduled**
*   **Sent**
*   **Outbox**
*   **Failed**
*   **Templates**

#### **All**

With "All" you can now view all the sent and received messages within a single view  
  

![Screen_Shot_2023-05-04_at_12.29.48_PM.png](./Text Conversation View – Salesmate_files/Screen Shot 2023-05-04 at 12.29.48 PM.png)

#### **Inbox**

*   You can view all the text messages which have received a reply. 

![Screen_Shot_2023-05-04_at_12.36.58_PM.png](./Text Conversation View – Salesmate_files/Screen Shot 2023-05-04 at 12.36.58 PM.png)

*   You can **Reply** to the Conversation from the relevant Conversation View

![Screen_Shot_2023-05-04_at_12.39.09_PM.png](./Text Conversation View – Salesmate_files/Screen Shot 2023-05-04 at 12.39.09 PM.png)

*   If you have multiple numbers assigned to single Contact, you can select the number on which you want to initiate the messaging
*   Insert the **Templates** from the Pre-defined list

![Screen_Shot_2023-05-04_at_12.54.35_PM.png](./Text Conversation View – Salesmate_files/Screen Shot 2023-05-04 at 12.54.35 PM.png)

*   **Schedule** the Text Message

![Screen_Shot_2023-05-04_at_12.58.07_PM.png](./Text Conversation View – Salesmate_files/Screen Shot 2023-05-04 at 12.58.07 PM.png)

*   When you hover over three dots beside the message, you will get the details of "To" and "From" numbers and can also delete the text.

![Screen_Shot_2023-05-04_at_1.00.40_PM.png](./Text Conversation View – Salesmate_files/Screen Shot 2023-05-04 at 1.00.40 PM.png)

Here's how the details pop up will look like:

![Screen_Shot_2023-05-04_at_1.02.04_PM.png](./Text Conversation View – Salesmate_files/Screen Shot 2023-05-04 at 1.02.04 PM.png)

#### **Scheduled**

*   Contains all Text Conversations that are scheduled for delivery in the future. 
    *   You can check the schedule timing
    *   From **Actions** (three dots menu), you can -
        *   **Reschedule**
        *   **Edit**
        *   **Delete**
        *   Check **Details**

![Screen_Shot_2023-05-04_at_1.34.22_PM.png](./Text Conversation View – Salesmate_files/Screen Shot 2023-05-04 at 1.34.22 PM.png)

#### **Sent** 

If you are into Bulk Texting, you can check the status of Bulk messaging here

![Screen_Shot_2023-05-04_at_1.37.57_PM.png](./Text Conversation View – Salesmate_files/Screen Shot 2023-05-04 at 1.37.57 PM.png)

#### **Outbox** 

All messages scheduled for instant delivery are queued under this folder and sent in batches of small chunks.

#### **Failed**

You can check the status of Failed Messages here in this folder and retry sending message from the same screen to the contact.

![Screen_Shot_2023-05-04_at_1.39.54_PM.png](./Text Conversation View – Salesmate_files/Screen Shot 2023-05-04 at 1.39.54 PM.png)

Or you can try sending bulk message by selecting multiple contacts

![Screen_Shot_2023-05-04_at_1.45.23_PM.png](./Text Conversation View – Salesmate_files/Screen Shot 2023-05-04 at 1.45.23 PM.png)

#### **Templates**

*   You can create and manage text templates 

![Screen_Shot_2023-05-04_at_1.49.05_PM.png](./Text Conversation View – Salesmate_files/Screen Shot 2023-05-04 at 1.49.05 PM.png)

*   Filter them under Folders
*   Create New Folders from the "+" icon

![Screen_Shot_2023-05-04_at_1.49.58_PM.png](./Text Conversation View – Salesmate_files/Screen Shot 2023-05-04 at 1.49.58 PM.png)

*   Create New Templates - Click on **\+ New Template**

**![Screen_Shot_2023-05-04_at_1.50.59_PM.png](./Text Conversation View – Salesmate_files/Screen Shot 2023-05-04 at 1.50.59 PM.png)**

*   Mention the **Template Name** (For identification and future reference)
*   Select the **Folder** in which the Text Template needs to be Saved
*   Provide the "Text Message" body to be sent
*   Insert Attributes - It can be used to Manage replaceable variables (Eg: {{Contact.firstName}} to replace First Name of Contact)

![Screenshot_2019-12-02_17.37.23.png](./Text Conversation View – Salesmate_files/Screenshot 2019-12-02 17.37.23.png)
