In Salesmate, you can unarchive a knowledge base article to restore it for public or internal access. Unarchiving brings the article back to its original state, making it accessible again.

<Note>
**Note:** You need **"Edit Articles"** permission to unarchive an article.
</Note>

### How to Unarchive an Article

*   Navigate to **Articles** from left side outreach under **More**.
*   If multiple knowledge bases exist, select the appropriate one.
*   Locate the article you want to **unarchive.**

<img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/47671119409945"   alt="Knowledge Base Articles List" />
*   Click on the **Actions** button and choose **Unarchive.**

<img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/47671836100377"   alt="Unarchive Knowledge Base Article" />
*   A confirmation dialog will appear:
    *   **"Are you sure you want to unarchive this article? It will be restored to its previous state."**
*   Click **Yes** to confirm.

<img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/47671836103065"   alt="Unarchive Confirmation Dialog" />
*   A success message will appear: **“Unarchived successfully.”**

<img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/47671877172377"   alt="Article Unarchived Successfully" />
<Note>
**Note:** The article status is changed to **Draft** when you unarchive an article.
</Note>
