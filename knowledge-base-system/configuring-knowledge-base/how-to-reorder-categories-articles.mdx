If you want to change the order in which categories and articles appear on your knowledge base portal, Salesmate makes it simple.

<Note>


**Note:** You need permission to organize categories for reordering the categories or articles.


</Note>

Topics Covered:

*   [How to Reorder the Categories](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/44524561708057-How-to-Reorder-Categories-Articles#h_01JSGV8AKDZ1SSXDAG0TY3ZVEQ)
*   [How to Reorder the Articles](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/44524561708057-How-to-Reorder-Categories-Articles#h_01JSHGQN9BZ096VJXD8521HZ37)

### How to Reorder the Categories

To Reorder the Categories,

*   Navigate to the **Profile Icon** from the top right corner.
*   Click on the **Setup**

<img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/47555168639385" alt="Salesmate Setup"/>
*   Head over to the **Library** category
*   Select the **Knowledge Base** option

<img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/47555168639769" alt="Knowledge Base Setup"/>
*   Click on the **Categories** tab
*   Simply drag and drop to reorder the **Categories**, which will be saved automatically.

<img rc="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/47555184797465" alt="Knowledge Base Reorder Categories"/>
### How to Reorder the Articles

To Reorder the Articles,

*   Navigate to the **Profile Icon** from the top right corner.
*   Click on the **Setup**

<img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/47555168639385" alt="Salesmate Setup"/>
*   Head over to the **Library** category
*   Select the **Knowledge Base** option

<img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/47555168639769" alt="Knowledge Base Setup"/>
*   You will see the subcategories and **Articles** in the **Category** you selected.

<img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/47555184797849" alt="Knowledge Base Category with Articles"/>
*   Navigate to the **Category** whose **Articles** you want to reorder.
*   Simply drag and drop to reorder the **Articles**, which will be saved automatically.

<img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/47555168641049" alt="Knowledge Base Reorder Articles"/>
