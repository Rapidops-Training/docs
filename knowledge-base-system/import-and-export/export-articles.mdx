The **Export Articles** feature allows you to download your knowledge base content into a CSV file. This is useful for data migration, backups, and reviewing your articles outside of the system.

<Note>
**Note:** Before you begin, make sure you have the necessary permission to export articles from the knowledge base.
</Note>

### How to Export Articles

#### **From the Knowledge Base**

*   Navigate to the **Profile Icon** on the top right corner
*   Click on the **Setup**
*   Head over to the  **Library**
*   Select **Knowledge Base**

<img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/48026059829145"   alt="Knowledge Base Setup Page" />
*   Click the **Actions** button and select **Export Articles**.
    

<img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/48026073766553"   alt="Export Articles from Knowledge Base" />
#### **From Setup**

*   Navigate to the **Profile Icon** at the top right corner
*   Click on **Setup**
*   Head over to the **Manage** **Data**
*   Click on **Export Data**

<img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/48026073767449"   alt="Export Data from Setup" />
*   Click on **Articles.**

<img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/48026059833625"   alt="Select Articles to Export" />
*   Choose the **View** and **Knowledge Base** you want to export.
    
*   Click the **Export** button.
    

<img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/48026059835929"   alt="Export Articles Settings" />
A message will appear saying:

*   *   _"We are preparing your export."_
        
    *   _"It may take a while, but we’ll notify you once the export is ready."_
        

<img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/48026134460185"   alt="Export in Progress Notification" />
*   Once the export is ready, you’ll receive a **bell notification** to download your file.

<img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/48026120549529"   alt="Export Ready Notification" />
### Export Customization Options

When exporting from the article listing or setup, you can customize what you want to include:

*   Choose which **fields** to export.
    
*   Use **“Select All”** to include all available fields or search and select them individually.
    
*   Rearrange selected fields in the order you'd like them to appear in the CSV.
    
*   **At least one field** must be selected to proceed.
    

<img src="https://support.salesmate.io/hc/article_attachments/48026059837977"   alt="Select Fields to Export" />
Only the fields visible to you will be included in the export. Fields that you don’t have access to won’t appear in the file.

### What’s Included in the Export File?

The exported CSV file may include the following columns, based on your selection:

*   **Slug**: A URL-friendly version of the article title, used to generate the article’s web address.
    
*   **Title**: The headline or main title of the article.
    
*   **Category**: The classification or section the article belongs to (e.g., Support, Product, FAQ).
    
*   **Article Body**: The full content or text of the article.
    
*   **Tags**: Keywords or labels associated with the article for easier searching and filtering.
    
*   **Language**: The language in which the article is written (e.g., English, Spanish).
    
*   **SEO Title**: A custom title used specifically for search engine optimization, different from the article title if needed.
    
*   **Meta Description**: A short summary used by search engines to describe the article in search results.
    

<Note>


### Notifications & Logs

*   Once the export is ready, you’ll receive a notification with a link to download the file.
    
*   If any issues occur, the notification will include details of the errors.
    
*   All export actions are logged, including who exported the file, which articles were included, and when it happened.
    


</Note>
