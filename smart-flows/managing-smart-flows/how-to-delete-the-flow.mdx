To delete a journey, please follow these steps:

- Navigate to the**Outreach Icon**from the left menu bar

- Click on the**Smart Flow**![](./How to Delete the Flow – Salesmate_files/mceclip0.png)

- Find your Flow.

- Click on the**Actions**button.

- Select the**Delete**option.

![](./How to Delete the Flow – Salesmate_files/mceclip1.png)

- Confirm your action to delete the Smart Flow.

![](./How to Delete the Flow – Salesmate_files/mceclip2.png)

Or

![](./How to Delete the Flow – Salesmate_files/mceclip0(1).png)

<Note>
  **Note:** On delete, all enrolled contacts to that Smart Flow get unenrolled.
  Delete operations are irreversible; once done, you can't recover your Smart
  Flow. If you want to stop any flow temporarily, then use the Pause or Lock
  flow options.
</Note>
