###**Business Type:**Ideal for businesses of all types looking to streamline meeting processes and enhance communication with participants for example**SaaS.**###**Template Type:**Perfect for teams managing internal or external meetings, providing automated confirmations and timely reminders.


###**Use Case:**This template simplifies the process of meeting confirmations and ensures participants are informed and prepared, enhancing communication and attendance rates.


###**Key Features of the Flow:*****Automated Meeting Confirmation:***   Sends a confirmation email once a meeting is scheduled.

    *   Automatically provides meeting details and access instructions.


***Timely Reminders:***   Sends multiple reminders leading up to the meeting.

    *   Ensures participants are informed and prepared for the meeting.


***Customizable Notifications:***   Customizable confirmation emails for branding and personalization.

    *   Reminders tailored to suit business needs and meeting types.



###**Steps Used in Creating the Flow:**You can [click here](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/36752706603929-How-to-Create-New-Smart-Flow) to learn how to create a new Smart Flow or access the Flow Templates.


***Trigger: [Record Created](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/36763966875289)*****Description**: The flow starts when a Meeting (Activity) is scheduled from the Meeting Scheduler containing a specific Title and Type
    ***Practical Usecase:**Whenever a meeting (Activity) is scheduled by the contact using your meeting scheduler and configured conditions are met then this flow will be triggered.

![Screenshot 2025-02-12 at 3.33.50 PM.png](./Custom Meeting Confirmation – Salesmate_files/Screenshot 2025-02-12 at 3.33.50 PM.png)


***Send Email Confirmation and Reminder: [Send Flow Email](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/38138164432409)*****Description**: The flow will now perform various Send Email action to send out Meeting Confirmation email and Reminder Emails before the due date

![Screenshot 2025-02-12 at 3.34.22 PM.png](./Custom Meeting Confirmation – Salesmate_files/Screenshot 2025-02-12 at 3.34.22 PM.png)


***Configure Wait to add delay between the Reminders: [Wait](https://support.salesmate.io/hc/en-us/articles/38138356545433)*****Description:**The flow will add a delay between the Send Flow Email actions to send reminders at 3 days, 2 days,s and 2 Hours.
    ***Practical Usecase:**This action is useful when you wish to add an interval between 2 actions being performed. For instance, you are sending a confirmation email and after 2 days you want to send a reminder email then this action is useful

![Screenshot 2025-02-12 at 3.34.36 PM.png](./Custom Meeting Confirmation – Salesmate_files/Screenshot 2025-02-12 at 3.34.36 PM.png)


###**Breakup of Items Used in the Flow:****Trigger:*****Meeting Created:**Initiates the flow when a new meeting is created in the system.**Actions:*****Send Confirmation Email:**Automatically sends a meeting confirmation email with event details.


***Reminder Sequence (Wait & Send Emails):*****Wait:**Pauses the flow until specific time conditions are met before the meeting.

    ***Send Flow Email:**Sends timely reminders:

        *   3 Days and 1 Day Before the Meeting

        *   1 Hour Before the Meeting**End:**The flow concludes after all reminders and confirmations are sent, ensuring participants are informed and prepared for the meeting.


###**Benefits:*****Improved Attendance:**Consistent reminders ensure participants remember and attend.


***Enhanced Communication:**Automated yet personalized messages reduce manual follow-ups.


***Operational Efficiency:**Reduces manual tasks for meeting confirmations and reminders.
