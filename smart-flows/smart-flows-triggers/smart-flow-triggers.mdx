---
title: "Smart Flow Triggers"
---
- **Smart Flow Triggers** in Salesmate are events that automatically start a Smart Flow. They act as the starting point for the automation process, allowing you to set up workflows that react when certain Conditions are met, to specific changes or Actions within your CRM **Note:** In Smart Flows, you can only use one trigger per flow.

###  Types of Triggers Available within Smart Flows

- [CRM](#crm)
- [Event](#event)
- [Utilities](#utilities)
- [Communication](#communication)
- [E-Commerce](#e-commerce)
